WIPEOUT

One wrong answer and you're out. How far can you go?

🔗
How to play
The last letter of a correct answer gives you the first letter of the next correct answer. All three answer options share the same starting letter — so you always know which letter you're working with!
📋
10 questions, one shot
You'll face 10 geography questions with 3 options each. There are no second chances — the game ends the moment you get one wrong.
⏱
10 seconds per question
If the timer hits zero before you answer, the game ends immediately!
📈
Difficulty increases as you go
Questions start Easy and get progressively harder. By question 9 you'll be in Expert territory — expect questions that will genuinely test you.
🏆
Your goal
Answer all 10 questions correctly within the time limit. Most people don't make it past question 7 — can you go all the way?
